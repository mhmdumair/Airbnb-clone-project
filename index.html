About Airbnb

Airbnb is an online marketplace that connects people who want to rent out their property with people who are looking for accommodations, typically for short stays. Airbnb offers hosts a relatively easy way to earn some income from their property. Guests often find that Airbnb rentals are cheaper and homier than hotels.Airbnb is a community built on a sharing economy. It is an online platform that allows property owners to list their place as holiday accommodation and allows travellers to find a place to stay while they are away from home.
